About Us

WHO-GSDP Certified Global Pharmaceutical Supplier

Medraw Pharmaceuticals is a WHO-GSDP certified, globally trusted pharmaceutical supplier based in Mumbai, India. Committed to improving healthcare access worldwide, Medraw serves hospitals, government bodies, clinical research organisations, healthcare institutions, and licensed medical practitioners by providing over 285,000 pharmaceutical product lines — including specialty, orphan, and hard-to-access medicines — to more than 180 countries across the globe.

Services

Comprehensive Solutions Beyond Supply

Named Patient Supply

We support patients with rare and life-threatening conditions by providing access to medicines to meet their treatment needs, including those not approved or commercially available in their home countries.

Clinical Trials Supply

We supply comparator drugs, rescue medications, co-medications, and investigational medicinal products (IMPs) with full documentation and global shipping to support clinical trials from start to finish.

Orphan Drug Supply

We supply orphan drugs and rare disease medicines globally through Named Patient Programs, compassionate use, and parallel import pathways — connecting patients with critical therapies wherever they are.

Government Supply Services

We participate in public tenders and provide direct supply of critical medicines to government hospitals, health departments, and non-profit programs, ensuring access to essential treatments.

Hospital & Healthcare Supplies

We source and distribute a comprehensive range of premium medical devices, surgical consumables, diagnostic kits, and patient care equipment to support modern healthcare facilities.

Laboratory Equipment Supplies

We provide state-of-the-art laboratory instrumentation, analyzers, centrifuges, and microscopes to research laboratories, clinical diagnostics centers, and academic institutions.

Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients

We supply high-purity APIs, drug substances, and excipients sourced from GMP-certified facilities to support international formulation developments and manufacturing needs.

Glassware and Chemicals

We deliver premium laboratory glassware (beakers, flasks, pipettes) and high-grade chemicals, reagents, and solvents meeting analytical and pharmacopoeial standards.

Reference Standards

We provide certified reference materials, pharmacopoeial standards (USP, EP, BP), and impurity standards to ensure rigorous quality control and analytical testing compliance.

Specialization

Validated Cold Chain Shipment

Our cold chain service protects pharmaceuticals by maintaining strict temperature control during transit. We use validated packaging, qualified logistics, and continuous monitoring to ensure every shipment meets compliance and quality standards.

Validated Packaging

Our packaging solutions are scientifically tested and qualified to maintain target temperatures for extended durations, ensuring product safety from pickup to delivery.

Global Compliance

Every shipment aligns with GDP, GMP, and international health authority guidelines to ensure traceability, documentation, and regulatory assurance.

In-Transit Temperature Monitoring

Shipments are continuously tracked using data loggers that monitor and record temperature conditions throughout transit, ensuring full visibility and compliance.
